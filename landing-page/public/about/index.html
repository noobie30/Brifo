About Brifo

Brifo is an AI meeting notes application for macOS. It auto-captures your meetings on any platform (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack Huddles, Discord) and generates structured AI notes, action items, and follow-up emails — without a bot joining the call.

What Brifo does

Brifo runs as a native macOS app plus a cloud backend. When a meeting starts on your Mac, Brifo detects it automatically, captures the system audio locally, sends the transcript to the cloud for AI processing, and produces a structured note with:

  • A meeting summary
  • Key decisions
  • Action items (one-click-filed to Jira)
  • A suggested follow-up email draft

Because Brifo captures system audio locally, no bot joins your meeting. Participants see nothing unusual, and no browser extension is required.

Who Brifo is for

Brifo is built for knowledge workers who live in meetings — product managers, engineering managers, founders, consultants, sales, and operators. If you spend 4+ hours a day on Zoom / Meet / Teams and your notes always lag behind, Brifo is for you.

How Brifo is different

  • No bot in the meeting. Unlike Otter.ai or Fireflies.ai, Brifo does not join your call. It captures system audio locally on your Mac.
  • Auto-detection. Unlike Granola, which requires a manual start, Brifo detects meetings automatically.
  • Jira out of the box. Action items become Jira tickets in one click.
  • Privacy-first. Audio is processed locally and never uploaded. Only the transcript goes to the cloud.

Technical details

  • Platform: macOS 13+ (Apple Silicon)
  • Desktop: Electron 33 + React 19
  • Backend: NestJS 10 + MongoDB
  • AI: OpenAI GPT-4.1 with deterministic fallback
  • Audio capture: macOS Core Audio Taps (since v0.1.5)
  • Integrations: Google Calendar, Jira (both OAuth)
