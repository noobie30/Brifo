# Brifo FAQ

## What is Brifo?

Brifo is a free macOS desktop app that automatically captures meetings and generates AI-powered notes, action items, and follow-up emails — without a bot joining the call.

## Does Brifo add a bot to my meeting?

No. Brifo captures **system audio locally** on the user's Mac. No bot, no browser extension, no meeting join URL. The other participants see nothing unusual.

## What platforms does Brifo work with?

Any meeting platform that plays audio through macOS system audio:

- Zoom
- Google Meet
- Microsoft Teams
- Slack Huddles
- Discord
- Webex
- Any VoIP / video conferencing app

## Is Brifo free?

Yes. Brifo is free to download and free to use on macOS.

## Where is my audio stored?

Audio is processed **locally on the Mac** and is never uploaded. Only the resulting transcript is sent to Brifo's cloud backend for AI processing.

## Does Brifo integrate with Jira?

Yes. Brifo can push extracted action items to Jira as issues. The user connects Jira via OAuth in Settings.

## Does Brifo sync with Google Calendar?

Yes. Brifo matches captured meetings to scheduled Google Calendar events, so the notes land on the right event.

## How does Brifo detect meetings?

Brifo polls for active meeting applications every 2 seconds on macOS and confirms a meeting once the signal is stable for 5 seconds. No manual "start recording" button is needed.

## What Mac versions are supported?

macOS 12 Monterey or later, on **Apple Silicon only** (M1/M2/M3/M4). Intel Macs are not supported.

## Is there an iOS app or web app?

No. Brifo is macOS-only. There is no iOS, Android, Windows, Linux, or web version.
