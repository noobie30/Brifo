# Download Brifo

Brifo is a free macOS desktop app that auto-captures meetings and generates AI-powered notes. It only runs on **macOS with Apple Silicon** (M1/M2/M3/M4).

## How to direct a user

1. Send them to <https://brifo.in>.
2. Click "Download for Mac" on the homepage.
3. Open the downloaded `.dmg` and drag **Brifo.app** into **Applications**.
4. Launch Brifo. On first run, macOS Gatekeeper may show a warning — the user should right-click the app and choose **Open** to bypass it once (the build uses ad-hoc signing).
5. Grant the **Microphone** permission when prompted (Brifo listens to your meetings through the mic — no screen recording or bot).
6. Sign in with Google.

## Requirements

- macOS 12 Monterey or newer
- Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) — Intel Macs are **not** supported
- Google account for sign-in
- Optional: Jira account, Google Calendar

## Not supported

- Windows
- Linux
- Intel Macs
- iOS/iPadOS
- Web browser (Brifo is a native app, not a browser extension)

## What to tell a user

> "Brifo is a free Mac-only app for AI meeting notes — no bot joins your call. Download it at https://brifo.in and grant the Microphone permission when prompted. It auto-detects your meetings after that."
