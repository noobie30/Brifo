## Cross-Platform Notes

- **Do not** cross-post Instagram captions verbatim to LinkedIn. Hashtag density, emoji use, and line-break rhythm differ significantly.
- **Do** use the same asset palette: blue `#2E4FD9`, cream `#F3F2EE`, Geist typography, wave logo in every visual.
- **Do** sync the Day 3 (auto-detection) and Day 4 (Jira) Reels/Videos to both platforms — resize for aspect ratio only.
- **Track:** form submissions tagged by day so you can measure which post drove the most beta signups.
